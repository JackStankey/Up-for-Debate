Key Debate Topics

Economic Policy

Van Jones Position

Advocates for progressive taxation, investment in green jobs, and robust social safety nets to reduce inequality.

Scott Jennings Position

Supports lower taxes, deregulation, and free market solutions to drive economic growth and business expansion.

Climate Change

Van Jones Position

Emphasizes urgent action on climate through renewable energy transition and environmental regulations.

Scott Jennings Position

Balances environmental concerns with energy independence and economic considerations in policy decisions.

Healthcare

Van Jones Position

Advocates for universal healthcare access and stronger government role in ensuring equitable health coverage.

Scott Jennings Position

Supports market-based healthcare solutions with limited government intervention and individual choice emphasis.

Immigration

Van Jones Position

Supports inclusive immigration policies with pathways to citizenship and humanitarian protections.

Scott Jennings Position

Emphasizes border security and managed immigration with strict enforcement of existing laws.

Education Reform

Van Jones Position

Calls for increased public school funding, equity initiatives, and eliminating educational disparities.

Scott Jennings Position

Advocates for school choice, parental control, and merit-based systems with competitive accountability.

Criminal Justice

Van Jones Position

Pursues systemic reform including police accountability, sentencing reform, and rehabilitation focus.

Scott Jennings Position

Prioritizes public safety, law enforcement support, and maintaining strong deterrence against crime.
