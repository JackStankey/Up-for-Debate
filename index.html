The Matchup

Van Jones

PROGRESSIVE VOICE

Van Jones is a three-time NY Times bestselling author and host of two CNN shows: "The Van Jones Show" and "The Redemption Project."

A leading voice in criminal justice reform, he serves as CEO of the REFORM Alliance, founded by Jay-Z and Meek Mill to transform the criminal justice system.

Jones previously served as a senior advisor to President Barack Obama and is widely regarded as a left-of-center political figure.

Scott Jennings

CONSERVATIVE VOICE

Scott Jennings is a prominent Republican strategist, CNN senior political commentator, and co-founder of the public relations firm RunSwitch.

A former special assistant to President George W. Bush, and a veteran of four presidential campaigns, Jennings provides conservative analysis on-air for CNN.

He contributes opinion pieces to USA Today and the LA Times, often described as a "black sheep" conservative providing nuanced Republican perspectives.

Key Debate Topics

Healthcare & Public Health

The future of public health policy and healthcare access in America.

Van Jones - Progressive Perspective

"Healthcare is a human right. We need to ensure every American has access to affordable, quality healthcare regardless of zip code or income level."

Scott Jennings - Conservative Perspective

"Market-based solutions and competition drive innovation and lower costs. We should focus on reducing regulatory burden and empowering consumers."

Women's Health & Rights

Women's autonomy, reproductive health, and workplace equality.

Van Jones - Progressive Perspective

"Women deserve freedom to make their own decisions about their bodies. Equal pay for equal work is essential for gender equity."

Scott Jennings - Conservative Perspective

"We should protect life while supporting women's economic advancement. Policies that strengthen families benefit all Americans."

Immigration & Border Security

Border security, immigration policy, and America's relationship with immigration.

Van Jones - Progressive Perspective

"America is built on immigration. We need humane policies that provide pathways to citizenship while maintaining security."

Scott Jennings - Conservative Perspective

"Strong borders are essential for national security and the rule of law. We must enforce existing laws while creating legal pathways."

Economic Opportunity & Inequality

Addressing economic inequality and creating pathways to prosperity.

Van Jones - Progressive Perspective

"We must address systemic inequalities. Investments in education, infrastructure, and small business create real opportunity."

Scott Jennings - Conservative Perspective

"Economic growth comes from reducing government barriers. Lower taxes and smart deregulation expand opportunity for working families."

Democracy, Media & Civic Trust

Strengthening democratic institutions, media integrity, and public trust.

Van Jones - Progressive Perspective

"Democracy requires an informed citizenry and free press. We must combat misinformation while protecting free speech."

Scott Jennings - Conservative Perspective

"Election integrity and constitutional order must be protected. We need election security and a media landscape respecting diverse viewpoints."

Foreign Policy & Global Role

America's position in the world and international responsibilities.

Van Jones - Progressive Perspective

"America's global leadership requires diplomacy and alliance-building. We must address climate change and promote human rights multilaterally."

Scott Jennings - Conservative Perspective

"America First means protecting our interests and citizens. We should maintain strong alliances but not overextend abroad. Peace through strength."
